## Description

MANTA targets anomaly detection for tiny objects, where anomalous regions can be small and difficult to localise. The dataset uses multi-view imagery and visual-text annotations to support models that combine perception with language-guided reasoning.

The visual component contains more than 137.3K images across 38 object categories and five views, including more than 8.6K anomalous images with pixel-level annotations. It also includes text resources such as declarative anomaly knowledge and image-text multiple-choice questions for anomaly reasoning.

MANTA was introduced as a large-scale multi-view visual-text benchmark for tiny-object anomaly detection.
