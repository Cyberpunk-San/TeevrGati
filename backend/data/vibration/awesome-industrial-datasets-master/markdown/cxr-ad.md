## Description

CXR-AD focuses on industrial anomaly detection using X-ray imagery rather than ordinary RGB inspection. The dataset contains component X-ray images across five categories, enabling evaluation of anomaly detection methods in non-destructive testing settings.

The dataset includes 653 normal and 561 anomalous images, with pixel-level masks for defective regions. It is intended for image-level anomaly detection, anomaly localisation, and segmentation under the imaging characteristics of industrial X-ray inspection.

CXR-AD was introduced in an arXiv paper in 2025 as a publicly accessible benchmark for component X-ray anomaly detection.
